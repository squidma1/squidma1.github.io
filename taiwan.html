Volunteering in Taiwan

I spent March 13th through May 20th of 2023 volunteering at an elementary school in Shanshang District, Tainan, Taiwan. I spoke English with the kids and helped in art, English, and music classes. Some of the projects I did while there include making an English version of the school's website, painting a mural on a wall by the parking lot, helping to translate an original book that they wrote about their town, and teaching kids how to read "Are You My Mother" by P.D. Eastman and recording their performance. The English website is not online yet, but the code cane be found on my GitHub.
