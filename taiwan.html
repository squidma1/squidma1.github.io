Volunteering in Taiwan

I spent March 13th through May 20th of 2023 volunteering at an elementary school in Shanshang District, Tainan, Taiwan. I spoke English with the kids and helped in art, English, and music classes. Some of the projects I did while there include making an English version of the school's website, painting a mural on a wall by the parking lot, helping to translate an original book that they wrote about their town, and teaching kids how to read "Are You My Mother" by P.D. Eastman and recording their performance.

These are some screenshots of the website that I made for the school. The website is not online yet, but once it is I will add the link here. The source code can be found on my GitHub.
